How This Keeps a Calendar Conflict-Free

Every meeting type gets its own color so conflicts are visible at a glance. Buffer time is built in after every client call to allow for follow-up notes before the next commitment. Protected focus blocks are treated as real meetings — not left open for anyone to book over. Every scheduling request is logged with proposed times and status, so nothing gets confirmed twice or forgotten in a group chat.
