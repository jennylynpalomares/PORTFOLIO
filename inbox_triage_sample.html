How This Keeps an Inbox Under Control

Every email gets sorted into one of four categories within minutes of arrival — Urgent, FYI, Delegate, or Archive. Urgent items get a same-day response SLA. FYI items are read, filed, and never reopened. Delegated items are routed immediately to the right person with context attached, instead of sitting in a shared inbox waiting for someone to notice. The result: zero unread by end of day, and nothing time-sensitive slips through.
